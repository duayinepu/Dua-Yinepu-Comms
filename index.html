About Me

I am a 3D artist working primarily in Blender, Substance Painter, and Unity.


I am familiar with the workflow for editing pre-existing avatars, kitbashing avatars together, redoing or creating new UV maps, weight painting, sculpting, creating props, complete retexture work, and avatar/shader setup in Unity.


I also offer VRChat photography sessions. Photos are shot in 4K using VRCLens and include editing.
